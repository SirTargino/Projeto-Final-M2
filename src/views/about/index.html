KAN.i is a technology startup whose mission is to help people with Autistic Spectrum Disorder too learn more easily and to include them more in the world of technology using mainly augmented reality resources with professionals specialized in Austistic.

Initially, the focus of the project is on teenagers between 12 and 21 years old, but with a hook to alsoo serve children, adults and the elderly in the future, after all, technoology is for everyone.

The entire internal environment was designed with a focus on the development of the students using the best of technooloogy today, seeking to bring that futuristic and technological air.
